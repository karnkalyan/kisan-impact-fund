- [ ] Confirm registered company name, entity type, registration details and address.
- [ ] Verify board and advisor names, roles, portrait permissions and biographies.
- [ ] Verify 49% / 31% / 20% proposed ownership structure.
- [ ] Verify strategic partner ranges and farmer promoter share concept.
- [ ] Approve all targets, projections, subscriber figures, income goals and IPO ambition.
- [ ] Confirm Kisan Net operating claims, offices, infrastructure and farmer-record count.
- [ ] Review every portfolio status and sector description.
- [ ] Approve investment disclaimer and risk disclosure under Nepal law.
- [ ] Approve privacy, cookies, terms, ESG, grievance and accessibility policies.
- [ ] Approve forms, consent text, data controller details, retention and processors.
- [ ] Confirm regulatory permissions before accepting enquiries or investments.
- [ ] Approve English and Nepali content independently.
- [ ] Add verified email, phone, social and grievance contacts.
- [ ] Confirm copyright and publication permission for all supplied imagery.
- [ ] Review organization schema, canonical domain, sitemap and social card.
