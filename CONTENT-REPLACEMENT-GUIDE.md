# Content replacement guide

## Before publishing

1. Confirm company registration name, legal form, address, dates and approved spelling of every name.
2. Replace contact, office-hours, grievance and social-media placeholders.
3. Approve or remove each strategic target, share range, ownership percentage and timeline.
4. Replace demonstration articles, dates, charts, progress percentages and forms.
5. Approve Nepali translations with a qualified legal/corporate reviewer.

## Main content locations

- `app/data.ts`: statistics, people, ventures and FAQs.
- `app/Site.tsx`: page sections, legal drafts, contact information and form copy.
- `app/[[...slug]]/page.tsx`: page titles, descriptions, canonical links and social metadata.
- `app/layout.tsx`: organization schema.

Keep initiative status labels accurate: **Flagship**, **Planned**, **Under Evaluation**, or **Strategic Sector**. Do not imply that a proposal is operating, regulated, approved or available for investment unless verified.
